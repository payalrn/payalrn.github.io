Write-up

The dataset that I use shows the water level of Lake Huron over the years. It has data from the year 1875 through 1972. I have added in a link of where to find the dataset in the introduction as well as in the form of an in-line link.

I did wish to add more interactivity to this data and add a chart as a built-in component as well but I talked to Xinhui on Slack and she said that it might be issues with the data set that made it not work.

I wanted to add a select component with a range of 10 years linked with a plot which would make it easy to understand than to have data of a 100 years put together.

I also wanted to add an image of the lake but it was difficult to find images of the SVG format.

I also added a preview of the Idyll built-in components page.
